Highland Meadows Farm

We're a third-generation family farm nestled in the Scottish Highlands, where our Shetland sheep roam freely across ancient pastures. Our commitment to traditional, ethical farming means every fleece tells a story of care, heritage, and respect for the land.

Our Story

What started as my grandfather's small flock in 1952 has grown into a beloved family tradition. Today, we maintain a flock of 120 heritage Shetland sheep, each known by name. We believe in slow farming – no shortcuts, no compromises.
